How to find affordable housing in Washington, Georgia

Different types of affordable housing in Washington, Georgia


There are many types of affordable housing in Washington, but some of the most common are HUD-assisted living facilities, public housing, and Section 8 housing.

HUD-assisted living facilities are typically senior citizen homes or disabled housing that is backed by the US Department of Housing and Urban Development. These homes are often very affordable, and many have waiting lists due to the high demand.

Public housing is another type of affordable housing that is administered by local housing authorities. These homes are typically for low-income families, and the rent is based on income.

Section 8 housing is a type of housing assistance that is given to low-income families, disabled individuals, and the elderly. This assistance comes in the form of a voucher that helps to pay for a portion of the rent.
