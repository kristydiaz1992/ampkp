Affordable Housing In Waukegan , Illinois

Waukegan Housing Authority provides safe and affordable housing for low

The Waukegan Housing Authority is committed to providing safe, affordable housing for low-income residents of Waukegan, Illinois. We are proud to offer a variety of housing options that meet the needs of our community. Our goal is to provide quality housing that is accessible to all, and to help our residents build strong, healthy families and communities.

When it comes to affordable housing, the city of Waukegan is working hard to provide options for low- and moderate-income families. In fact, the city has created more affordable housing units in recent years to combat homelessness.

For seniors, the city offers affordable housing options that make it possible to age in place. For families, there are developments that provide safe, clean, and affordable homes. And for those who are struggling with mental health or addiction, there are supportive housing units available.

Thanks to the city's affordable housing initiatives, residents are finding hope and stability. And that is making a difference in the lives of individuals and families throughout Waukegan.
