Affordable Housing In Longmont , Colorado

Affordable Housing in Longmont

, Colorado

As the cost of living in Colorado continues to rise, many people are finding it difficult to find affordable housing. The City of Longmont is addressing this problem by working to create more affordable housing options.

The City of Longmont has adopted an inclusionary housing ordinance which requires new developments of 30 or more units to set aside a certain number of units for people with low or moderate incomes. The ordinance also provides incentives for developers who create even more affordable units.

The City is also working to create more opportunities for homeownership through down payment assistance programs and working with developers to create more starter homes.

In addition to these efforts, the City is also working to increase the supply of affordable rental housing. The City is providing funding to organizations that develop and manage affordable rental housing and is also working to create more regulations that will prevent landlords from unfairly raising rents.

The City of Longmont is committed to creating more affordable housing options for its residents. These efforts will help to ensure that everyone has a place to call home.
