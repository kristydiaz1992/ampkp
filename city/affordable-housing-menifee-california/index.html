The Different Types of Affordable Housing in Menifee





There are many types of affordable housing in Menifee. Here are some of the most common:

1. Low Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC)

This type of affordable housing is available to low-income individuals and families. The LIHTC program provides tax credits to developers who build or rehabilitate housing that is affordable to low-income households.

2. Housing Choice Vouchers

Housing choice vouchers are available to low-income families, the elderly, and the disabled. These vouchers can be used to rent private housing that meets certain standards.

3. Public Housing

Public housing is available to low-income families, the elderly, and the disabled. Public housing units are owned and managed by local public housing authorities.

4. Section 8 Project-Based Rental Assistance

Section 8 project-based rental assistance provides low-income families with the opportunity to live in privately owned and operated housing. The assistance is provided in the form of a subsidy, which is paid directly to the landlord.

5. Section 202 Supportive Housing for the Elderly

The Section 202 program provides supportive housing for low-income elderly persons. The program provides subsidies to private landlords who make their units available to low-income elderly tenants.

6. Section 811 Supportive Housing for Persons with Disabilities

The Section 811 program provides supportive housing for low-income persons with disabilities. The program provides subsidies to private landlords who make their units available to low-income disabled tenants.
