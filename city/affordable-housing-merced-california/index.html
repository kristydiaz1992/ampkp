The current state of affordable housing in Merced and what needs to be improved


The current state of affordable housing in Merced is not good. There is a lack of affordable housing options, and the ones that do exist are often in poor condition. This is a problem for low-income families, as they may not be able to find housing that is affordable and safe. There are a few things that could be done to improve the situation.

One option is to create more affordable housing. This could be done by offering incentives to developers to build more affordable housing, or by creating public housing. Another option is to improve the quality of existing affordable housing. This could be done by investing in repairs and renovations, or by increasing the amount of affordable housing that is up to code.

Whatever the solution, it is clear that something needs to be done to improve the current state of affordable housing in Merced. Low-income families should not have to sacrifice their safety or quality of life in order to find a place to live.

What is being done to increase the amount of affordable housing in Merced?


There is a growing recognition that the lack of affordable housing is a serious problem in Merced and across the country. A number of initiatives are underway to try to increase the amount of affordable housing in our community.

One major effort is the city of Merced’s recent adoption of an inclusionary housing ordinance.
