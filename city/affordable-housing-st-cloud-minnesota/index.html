The Different Types of Affordable Housing Available in St. Cloud


There are many different types of affordable housing available in St. Cloud, Minnesota. Some of the most common types of affordable housing include public housing, Section 8 housing, and low-income housing.

Public housing is housing that is owned and operated by the government. Public housing is available to low-income families, the elderly, and people with disabilities. Section 8 housing is a program that provides housing assistance to low-income families. Section 8 housing is available through the government and through private landlords. Low-income housing is housing that is affordable for low-income families. Low-income housing is often available through the government, but there are also many private companies that offer low-income housing.
