Affordable Housing In Saint Louis Park , Minnesota

Construction begins on new affordable housing development in Saint Louis Park


Construction is underway on a new affordable housing development in Saint Louis Park. The development, located at the corner of Minnetonka Boulevard and Louisiana Avenue, will feature 48 units of affordable housing for families and individuals.

The development is a joint effort between the City of Saint Louis Park, the Minnesota Housing Finance Agency, and CommonBond Communities, a nonprofit affordable housing provider. CommonBond will manage and operate the development.

The City of Saint Louis Park is providing $3.5 million in financing for the project, which will be repaid through the sale of tax-exempt bonds. The Minnesota Housing Finance Agency is providing $5.5 million in low-income housing tax credits, which will generate approximately $4.5 million in equity for the project.

The total development cost is estimated at $13.5 million. Construction is expected to be completed in late 2019.

Affordable housing crisis in Saint Louis Park reaches breaking point

The situation has become so dire that some residents are being forced to move out of the city altogether. This is not only a loss for the city, but also for the businesses and organizations that rely on these residents’ support.

It’s time for the city to take action and find a way to address the affordable housing crisis before it’s too late.
