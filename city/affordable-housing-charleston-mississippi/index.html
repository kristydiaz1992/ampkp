What are the different types of affordable housing in Charleston, Mississippi?


There are many types of affordable housing in Charleston, Mississippi. Some of the most common include:

1. Public housing: This type of housing is funded by the government and is typically reserved for low-income families.

2. Low-income housing: This type of housing is typically privately owned but may receive government subsidies. It is typically reserved for low-income families.

3. Section 8 housing: This type of housing is privately owned but the government subsidizes the rent. It is typically reserved for low-income families.

4. Senior housing: This type of housing is typically privately owned but may receive government subsidies. It is typically reserved for seniors citizens.

5. Affordable housing: This type of housing is typically privately owned but may receive government subsidies. It is typically priced below the market rate and is available to families of all income levels.
