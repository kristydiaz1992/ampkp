What are some creative solutions to the affordable housing crisis in Silver Springs?



There is no easy or single answer to the affordable housing crisis in Silver Springs. However, some creative solutions that have been proposed include:

1. Establishing a Community Land Trust: This would create a pool of land that would be leased to developers for the production of affordable housing.

2. Establishing an Affordable Housing Fund: This would provide financing for the development of affordable housing projects.

3. Incentivizing Inclusionary Zoning: This would provide tax breaks or other incentives for developers who include a certain percentage of affordable units in their projects.

4. Requiring Affordable Housing in New Development: This would mandate that a certain percentage of units in new developments be set aside for affordable housing.

5. Increasing Funding for Public Housing: This would provide additional resources for the development and maintenance of public housing units.

6. Creating Mixed-Income Housing: This would involve the development of housing that includes units for people of different income levels.

7. Establishing Rent Control Policies: This would limit the amount of rent that landlords can charge, making housing more affordable for tenants.

8. Providing Housing Vouchers: This would give low-income households vouchers that could be used to help cover the cost of rent.

9.Building Accessory Dwelling Units: This would allow for the development of smaller, secondary units on existing properties.

10. Converting Hotels and Motels into Housing: This would provide additional housing units by repurposing existing commercial buildings.
