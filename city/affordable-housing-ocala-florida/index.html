The difference between affordable housing and low income housing



There is a big distinction between affordable housing and low income housing. Low income housing is usually government subsidized and is only available to those who meet certain income requirements. Affordable housing is not necessarily subsidized, but is still considered affordable because the rent or mortgage is at a lower price range than other properties in the area. This could be due to the location of the housing, the type of housing, or other factors.
