What are the different types of affordable housing in Altamonte Springs?


There are several different types of affordable housing in Altamonte Springs. The most common type is public housing, which is provided by the government. Other types of affordable housing include subsidized housing, cooperative housing, and private housing.

Public housing is owned and operated by the government, and it is typically reserved for low-income families. Subsidized housing is also available to low-income families, but it is not owned or operated by the government. Cooperative housing is a type of housing where residents own and manage the property. Private housing is any type of housing that is not owned or operated by the government.
