types of affordable housing in Littlefork, Minnesota


There are four types of affordable housing in Littlefork, Minnesota: public housing, subsidized housing, Section 8 housing, and low-income housing.

Public housing is owned and operated by the government, and is typically available to low-income families, the elderly, and people with disabilities. Subsidized housing is privately owned and operated, but the government provides subsidies to help make rent more affordable. Section 8 housing is a government-funded program that provides vouchers to low-income households to help pay for rent. Low-income housing is privately owned and operated, but is restricted to households that earn below a certain income level.
