What is affordable housing and why is it important?


Affordable housing is housing that is deemed affordable to those with a median income or lower. It is often owned or managed by a government agency, nonprofit organization, or private company. Affordable housing is important because it provides a safe and stable place for people to live who may not be able to afford market-rate housing. It can also help to create diverse and inclusive communities.
