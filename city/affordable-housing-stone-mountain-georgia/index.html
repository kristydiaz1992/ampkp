The different types of affordable housing available in Stone Mountain, Georgia



There are many types of affordable housing available in Stone Mountain, Georgia. Some of the most common types of affordable housing are:

1. Low-Income Housing: This type of affordable housing is typically reserved for households that earn below a certain income level. In most cases, low-income housing is government-subsidized in order to make it more affordable.

2. Senior Housing: Senior housing is typically more affordable than traditional housing options, as many seniors have a fixed income. There are many different types of senior housing available, including independent living, assisted living, and nursing home facilities.

3. Section 8 Housing: Section 8 housing is a federally-funded program that provides housing vouchers to low-income households. The voucher can be used to help pay for rent at a qualified apartment or home.

4. Public Housing: Public housing is owned and operated by local government agencies. In most cases, public housing is reserved for low-income households.

5. Affordable Housing Communities: There are many affordable housing communities that offer a variety of housing options, including apartments, townhomes, and single-family homes. These communities typically have income restrictions in place.
