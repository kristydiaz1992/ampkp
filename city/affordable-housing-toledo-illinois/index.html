The Different Types of Affordable Housing in Toledo


There are many different types of affordable housing in Toledo. Some of the most common are public housing, section 8 housing, and low income housing.

Public housing is government-owned and -operated housing for low-income residents. Section 8 housing is a federally-funded program that provides housing vouchers to low-income residents. Low income housing is privately-owned and -operated housing for low-income residents.

Each type of affordable housing has its own eligibility requirements, benefits, and drawbacks. Public housing is typically the most affordable, but it can be difficult to qualify for.
