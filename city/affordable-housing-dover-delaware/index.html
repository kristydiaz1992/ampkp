How Dover's affordable housing crisis is affecting residents

Many people are being forced to move out of the city because they can no longer afford the rent, which is becoming increasingly expensive. The lack of affordable housing is also causing problems for people who are still able to live in the city. For example, families are being forced to live in overcrowded conditions because they can’t afford to move to a bigger place. This can lead to health problems and other problems associated with living in cramped quarters.
