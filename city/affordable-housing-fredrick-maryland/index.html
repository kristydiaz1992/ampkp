7. A sense of community. When people live in affordable housing, they develop a sense of community. They get to know their neighbors and feel like they belong. This can lead to healthier, more active, and more engaged communities.

The benefits of affordable housing are clear. It’s time for Frederick, Maryland to invest in affordable housing for our families and our future.
