What is affordable housing and why is it important?


Affordable housing is housing that is deemed affordable to those with a median household income as specified by country, region, or municipality by a housing affordability index. Housing is considered affordable if the household pays no more than 30% of its gross income on housing costs, including utilities.

Why is affordable housing important? Affordable housing is important because it provides a basic human need – shelter. It is a social determinant of health and has been linked to improved health outcomes.
