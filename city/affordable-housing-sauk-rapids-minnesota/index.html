How to qualify for affordable housing in Sauk Rapids, Minnesota



If you're looking for affordable housing in Sauk Rapids, Minnesota, there are a few things you need to know. First, you must be income eligible. This means your household income must be at or below 80% of the Area Median Income (AMI).

Second, you must be a first-time homebuyer or have not owned a home in the past three years.

Third, you must be a U.S. citizen or legal resident alien.

Fourth, you must occupy the home as your primary residence.

Lastly, you must complete a homebuyer education course and obtain a certificate of completion.

If you meet all of the above criteria, you should contact the Minnesota Housing Finance Agency (MHFA) to learn more about affordable housing opportunities in Sauk Rapids.
