The Different Types of Affordable Housing Available


There are many types of affordable housing available to those who need it. Here are just a few of the most common options:

Public Housing: This is housing that is owned and managed by the government. It is usually reserved for low-income families and individuals, although there may be some income restrictions in place.

Section 8 Housing: Also known as the Housing Choice Voucher program, this is a federally-funded program that helps low-income families pay for housing. Participants are given vouchers that they can use to rent units from participating landlords.

Low-Income Housing Tax Credit: This is a federal tax credit that helps developers build or rehabilitate affordable housing units. It is reserved for developments that serve low- and moderate-income households.

There are many other types of affordable housing programs available, both at the federal and state level. If you are in need of affordable housing, it is important to research all of your options to find the best fit for your needs.
