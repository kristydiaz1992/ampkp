1. Increase density.

3. Allow accessory dwelling units.

Accessory dwelling units (ADUs) are small housing units that are built on the same property as a single-family home. They can be used as rental units, or for family members who need a place to live. Allowing ADUs can help to increase the density of a community, and make housing more affordable.

One way to make housing more affordable is to increase the supply of affordable housing. This can be done by building more affordable housing units, or by providing incentives for developers to build more affordable housing.

6. Create community land trusts.

Community land trusts are nonprofit organizations that buy and hold land for the benefit of the community. They can be used to create permanently affordable housing, or to provide space for community gardens or other public amenities.

7. Improve public transportation.

Making it easier for people to get around without a car can help to make housing more affordable. This can be done by improving public transportation, or by creating more walkable and bikeable communities.

8. Increase funding for affordable housing.

One way to make housing more affordable is to increase funding for affordable housing. This can be done at the local, state, or federal level.

9. Provide incentives for developers to build affordable housing.

10. Advocate for change.
