The Benefits of Affordable Housing

Affordable housing can also lead to increased social mobility as people are able to move to areas with better schools and job opportunities. This can have a knock-on effect of reducing crime and improving overall community health.

Another key benefit of affordable housing is that it can help to reduce environmental pollution. This is because people who live in affordable housing are often less likely to own cars and so they rely more on public transport or walking/cycling. This reduces traffic congestion and helps to cut down on emissions.

Finally, affordable housing can help to create a sense of community and belonging. This is because people who live in affordable housing developments often know each other and look out for one another. This can lead to stronger social ties and a greater sense of community cohesion.
