What are the different types of affordable housing in Prairie City, Iowa


There are many types of affordable housing in Prairie City, Iowa. Some of the most common include public housing, Section 8 housing, and LIHTC housing.

Public housing is housing that is owned and operated by the government. It is typically reserved for low-income households. Section 8 housing is a program that provides subsidies to private landlords who rent to low-income households. LIHTC housing is housing that is financed through the Low-Income Housing Tax Credit program. This program provides tax credits to developers who build or rehabilitate housing for low-income households.
