The term “affordable housing” refers to housing that is available and affordable for low- and moderate-income households. In general, affordable housing costs no more than 30% of a household’s income.

Public housing is a type of affordable housing that is owned and operated by government entities such as local housing authorities. Public housing units are typically available to low-income households.
