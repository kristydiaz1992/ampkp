Affordable Housing In Norton , Massachusetts

The Norton Housing Authority provides quality affordable housing for eligible low

and moderate-income residents of the City of Norton, Virginia. The Authority operates 180 public housing units and manages an additional 354 units of privately-owned subsidized housing.

The Authority’s mission is to promote self-sufficiency among its residents through programs that encourage economic independence and continued education. The Authority also strives to provide a safe, clean and well-maintained living environment for all of its residents.
