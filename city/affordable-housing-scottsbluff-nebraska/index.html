The third challenge is that the lack of affordable housing can lead to increased segregation and isolation of low-income residents. This is because families who are struggling to pay for housing are more likely to experience financial stress, which can lead to other problems such as skipping meals, not being able to afford medical care, or turning to crime to make ends meet. Additionally, living in poor housing conditions can itself lead to health problems.
