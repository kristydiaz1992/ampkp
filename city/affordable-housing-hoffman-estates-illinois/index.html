What Are the Different Types of Affordable Housing?


There are many types of affordable housing, but the most common are public housing, Section 8 housing, and low-income housing.

Public housing is housing that is owned and operated by the government. It is usually reserved for low-income families and individuals, although there are some public housing units that are available for people with higher incomes.

Section 8 housing is a program that provides financial assistance to low-income families so that they can afford to rent housing in the private market. Families who participate in the Section 8 program pay a portion of their rent, and the government pays the rest.

Low-income housing is housing that is affordable for families and individuals with low incomes. There are many different types of low-income housing, including government-subsidized housing, housing that is reserved for low-income families, and housing that is available for people with very low incomes.
