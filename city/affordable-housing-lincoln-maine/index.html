The benefits of affordable housing

3. reduced crime
4. improved health outcomes
5. increased social cohesion

One of the most obvious benefits of affordable housing is that it can improve the quality of life for residents. This is because residents are able to save money on their housing costs, which gives them more disposable income to spend on other things.

Increased economic activity

Another benefit of affordable housing is that it can lead to increased economic activity. This is because when people have more disposable income, they are more likely to spend it, which in turn boosts economic activity.

Reduced crime

Improved health outcomes

Another benefit of affordable housing is that it can lead to improved health outcomes. This is because when people have more disposable income, they are able to afford healthier food and are more likely to have access to healthcare.
