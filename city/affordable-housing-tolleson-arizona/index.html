The city is trying to attract more development to build more housing, but the high cost of land and the lack of infrastructure are making it difficult. The city is also working on a program to help families with their rent, but it is not enough to keep up with the demand.

The city is in a difficult situation, but there are ways to solve the affordable housing dilemma. The city needs to attract more development, provide more financial assistance to families, and create more incentives for developers to build affordable housing.
