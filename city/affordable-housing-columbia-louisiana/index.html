Section 8 housing



The Section 8 housing program is a government-funded program that provides housing assistance to low-income families and individuals. The program is administered by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD).

Under the program, participating families and individuals receive a voucher that they can use to rent a qualifying unit. The voucher covers a portion of the rent, with the family or individual responsible for paying the remainder.

The Section 8 program has helped millions of low-income families and individuals obtain affordable housing. The program has also been credited with reducing homelessness and improving the quality of life for participants.
