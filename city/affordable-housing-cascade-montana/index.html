11. What can you do to help create more affordable housing in Cascade?

Affordable housing is a top priority for the City of Cascade and we are committed to creating more opportunities for our residents. We know that safe, decent and affordable housing is essential for individuals and families to thrive.

We are working to create more affordable housing options for our residents through a variety of initiatives, including:

1. Working with partners to develop new affordable housing developments
2. Providing financial assistance to low- and moderate-income households to help with rent or homeownership costs
3. Encouraging the development of accessory dwelling units
4. Creating incentives for developers to include affordable housing in their projects
5. Educating our community about the importance of affordable housing and the available resources

You can help create more affordable housing in Cascade by:

1. Supporting initiatives to create more affordable housing
2. Sharing information about affordable housing resources with your friends and family
3. Volunteering with or donating to organizations that work to create more affordable housing
4. Supporting policies and regulations that promote the development of affordable housing
5. Educating yourself and others about the importance of affordable housing
