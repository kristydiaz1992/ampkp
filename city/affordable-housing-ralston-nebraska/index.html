The different types of affordable housing


There are many types of affordable housing, including public housing, rent-controlled apartments, housing vouchers, and co-ops.

Public housing is owned and operated by the government and is usually reserved for low-income families or individuals. Rent-controlled apartments are typically privately owned but have rent prices that are set below the market rate. Housing vouchers help low-income families or individuals pay for private housing. Co-ops are privately owned housing developments that are typically owned and operated by the residents.
