8 ways to make your small space feel like home

Furnishing your first apartment on a budget


Furnishing your first apartment on a budget can be a daunting task. There are so many things to consider and it can be hard to stay within your budget. However, with a little planning and creativity, you can furnish your first apartment without breaking the bank.

Here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Start with the basics. Furnishing your first apartment doesn’t have to mean buying all new furniture. Start with the basics like a bed, couch, and coffee table. If you already have these items, great! If not, look for used furniture or check out online stores like Wayfair or Amazon for affordable options.

2. Get creative with storage. When it comes to storage, think outside the box. Bookshelves can be used for more than just books. Use them to store items like towels, linens, or even non-perishable food items.

3. Consider multipurpose furniture. When choosing furniture for your first apartment, try to find pieces that can be used for more than one purpose. For example, an ottoman can be used as a coffee table or extra seating. A futon can be used as a couch during the day and a bed at night.

4. Add personal touches. One of the best ways to make your first apartment feel like home is to add personal touches. Hang up photos, artwork, or even string lights. Place a few plants around the apartment to add some life.

5. Shop around. Don’t just buy the first thing you see. Shop around for the best deals and compare prices. Don’t be afraid to bargain either. Many stores are willing to negotiate, especially if you’re buying multiple items.

With these tips, you can furnish your first apartment on a budget without sacrificing style or comfort.

5 hacks for living in a small space


If you live in a small space, you know the challenges that come with it. You might feel like you don’t have enough storage, you can’t entertain guests, or your space just feels cramped. But there are ways to make the most of your small space. Here are 5 hacks for living in a small space:

1. Use double-duty furniture.

One of the best ways to make the most of a small space is to use double-duty furniture. For example, use a ottoman as a coffee table and for extra seating. Or, use a dresser as a TV stand.

2. Get creative with storage.

There are lots of ways to get creative with storage in a small space. Use wall-mounted shelves to store books or display décor. Or, use bins and baskets to keep things organized.

3. Use mirrors to make the space look bigger.

Hang a mirror on the wall or use a floor mirror to make the space look bigger. Mirrors reflect light and make a space look more open.

4. Use light colors.

Paint the walls and ceiling a light color to make the space look bigger. Avoid dark colors, which can make a space feel smaller.

5. Use furniture with legs.

Furniture with legs looks lighter and takes up less visual space. So, use a glass coffee table or a chairs with slim legs.

By following these hacks, you can make the most of your small space.
