Nearly half of all renters in Cambridge spend more than 30% of their income on rent, and one in four spend more than 50% of their income on rent. The high cost of housing also makes it difficult for Cambridge residents to stay in the city. Affordable housing is important for Cambridge residents because it allows them to stay in the city and maintain their quality of life.
