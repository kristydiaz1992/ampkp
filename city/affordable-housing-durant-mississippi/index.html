types of affordable housing in Durant, Mississippi


There are several types of affordable housing in Durant, Mississippi. There are Low-Income Housing Tax Credit units, public housing units, and Section 8 housing vouchers. There are also Habitat for Humanity homes and other types of subsidized housing.

The public housing program is a federally funded program that provides housing for low-income families, the elderly, and people with disabilities. The Section 8 housing voucher program is a federally funded program that helps low-income families pay for rent. Habitat for Humanity is a nonprofit organization that builds and repairs homes for low-income families. There are also many state and local programs that provide affordable housing.
