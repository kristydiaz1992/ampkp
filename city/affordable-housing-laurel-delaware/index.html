What is "Affordable Housing" in Laurel, Delaware?


Affordable housing is housing that is deemed affordable to those with a median income or lower. In Laurel, Delaware, affordable housing is a housing unit that costs no more than 30% of the monthly income of a household. This can include public housing, vouchers, and other subsidy programs.
