How to make affordable housing more available


There are many ways to make affordable housing more available, but one of the most effective is to create incentives for developers to include affordable units in their projects. Inclusionary zoning ordinances, for example, require or encourage developers to set aside a certain percentage of units in a new development for low- and moderate-income households.
