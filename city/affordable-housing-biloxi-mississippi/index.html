Despite these efforts, the lack of affordable housing in Biloxi is still a major problem. It’s one that requires a multi-faceted approach to solve. The city, businesses, and philanthropic organizations all have a role to play in ensuring that everyone in Biloxi has a safe, decent, and affordable place to call home.
