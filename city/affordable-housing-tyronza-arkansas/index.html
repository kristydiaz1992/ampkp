Another creative way to make affordable housing more accessible is to allow people to build accessory dwelling units (ADUs) on their property. ADUs are small homes that are built on the same lot as a larger home. They can be used as a rental unit, guest house, or office space.

Allowing people to build ADUs can help to increase the supply of affordable housing in an area. It can also provide additional income for homeowners, which can help to make housing more affordable.
